Banana Bread Recipe

Description

If you're looking for a reliable website where you can find tasty recipes, then you're in the right place! This pastry dish is a delicous one that many people rave about, and personally it's my favorite, banana bread! The banana bread may seem a bit complicated to make, especially to those who aren't much of a baker themselves. However, this recipe will bring out the baker in you and will have you bragging to your friends and family about it. All it requires are 3 simples steps and 7 ingredients, all which will thoroughly explained later down the recipe. A loaf pan, an oven, and an appetite are also needed materials.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup multed butter
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (or 165 degrees celius)
  2. Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and butter together in a bowl. Then mix the flour and baking soda together in a seperate bowl and after stir into banana mixture until batter is mixed. Stir salt into batter. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  3. Now insert a toothpick in the center of the bread and bake until it comes out clean, which should be about an hour. Enjoy!
