Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 tablespoon of ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 2/3 milk
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter, or more if required

Steps

  1. Whisk the milk, eggs, vanilla, and salt together inside a bowl
  2. Lightly butter the skillet and heat over medium-high heat
  3. Now dunk the bread in the egg mixture, soaking in both sides of it. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3-4 minutes per side. Now ready to serve hot.
