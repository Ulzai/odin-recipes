Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

The best pizza recipe can mean something different to every person. Everyone has there own version of what that can be, however we're sure this one here is surely to be loved by all! The classic pepperoni pizza is the one you enjoy at birthday parties, when you're out bowling or when you crave it on your own one day! Now, making it can be challenging but not with the right materials and instructor. Here in this page, you'll learn everything you need to know in order to make your own pizza.

You will have 3 different sections that are compiled into their own ingredients for your convience. So prepare yourself and your oven, it's time to make pizza!

Ingredients

Pizza Sauce

  • 12 ounce can Contadina Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Pizza Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, or more if needed
  • 2 envelops FLEISHMANN'S Pizza Crust Yeast or RapidRise Yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/3 cups for hot water (120-130 degrees F to be exact)
  • 1/3 cup oil

Toppings

  • 1 package HORMEL Pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more for extra flavor

Steps

  1. For the sauce: Combine all sauce ingredients with a half cup water in a medium sized bowl. Set aside so flavors can set while preparing the crust
  2. For the crust: Combine 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast, sugar and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until blended (about a minute). Slowly add the remaining flour until a soft, sticky dough ball can be formed.
  3. Knead for about 4 minutes on a surface covered with flour, until dough is smooth and elastic. If needed add more flour. (If you're using RapidRise Yeast. let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)
  4. Divide dough in half. Pat each half (make sure to have hands covered in flour as well) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan or roll doll to fit inside the pans.
  5. For pizza: Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Top the center with sauce, cheese and pepperoni.
  6. Now bake for 18-20 minutes until crust are browned and cheese is bubbly. For best results, make sure to rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks half way through baking.
